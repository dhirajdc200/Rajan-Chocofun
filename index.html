Choco Fun

Chocolaty fun, crispy wafer delight !

As the first original chocolate wafer in the country, Chocofun has been the primary choice of children in Nepal for over 20 years and the advertising campaign 'Only Chocofun' aims to manifest the certainty of Chocofun adding more colours in the happy moments of children's lives.

About Choco Fun

Chocofun is a chocolate enrobed wafer consisting 3 layers of crunchy and crispy wafer, which is combined together by and enrobed with rich chocolate cream. It is delighting its consumers with its crispy, crunchy wafer and delicious chocolaty taste since 1999 and has established itself as a leading confectionery brand of the country.


Why Choose ChocoFun?

Experience the perfect blend of rich chocolate and crispy wafer in every bite. ChocoFun is crafted for moments of joy and indulgence. Whether you're treating yourself or sharing with friends, ChocoFun adds sweetness to any occasion.
